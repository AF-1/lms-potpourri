PLUGIN_POTPOURRI_STARTVOLUME
	EN	Player Power-On Volume

PLUGIN_POTPOURRI_ENABLE
	EN	Change player power-on volume

PLUGIN_POTPOURRI_ENABLE_DESC
	EN	<b>Disabled</b>: Do nothing when a player is powered on.<br><br><b>Set to preset value</b>: Set the volume of a player to a <i>preset</i> value of your choice when the player is turned on.<br><br><b>Set to last value</b>: Devices remember their previous volume level. If for some reason this not the case, setting the volume of a device to the last value <i>with this plugin</i> could be a workaround.

	EN	Preset volume

PLUGIN_POTPOURRI_PRESETVOLUME_DESC

PLUGIN_POTPOURRI_RAISE
	EN	Allow raising volume

PLUGIN_POTPOURRI_RAISE_DESC
	EN	If the <b>last</b> volume of a player was <b>below</b> the <b>preset</b> level when the player was turned off, leave it there rather than raising it.
